Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CommuteGeek collects only the information necessary to deliver our alarm and commute features, including:

  • Location data (for commute time estimation)
  • User-provided destinations and arrival times
  • Optional prep time settings

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide personalized alarm scheduling based on real-time commute data. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. All data is stored securely and transmitted using encrypted channels where applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party APIs (e.g., for traffic data) but do not share personally identifiable information with those services.

5. Your Choices

You can delete your data from within the app settings. You may also revoke location access through your device's system settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please email us at: info@commutegeek.com.
